Apple Watch — Health Data Import

Import your Apple Health data once a week. The system matches your heart rate, HRV, and sleep to your trading sessions automatically.

How It Works
1
Trade all week using the journal. Log your sessions, trades, and emotions here.
2
On the weekend, open Health app on iPhone → tap your profile icon (top right) → Export All Health Data → AirDrop the zip to your Mac.
3
Upload the zip file below. The system reads your HR, HRV, and sleep data and matches it to your trading session timestamps.
Upload Health Export
⇪
Drop export.zip here or click to browse
Accepts .zip or .xml
CLI Alternative

You can also import from the terminal:

python3 health_import.py ~/Desktop/export.zip
Pro Tip

Start an "Other" workout on your Apple Watch when you begin trading. This makes the watch record heart rate every 3-5 seconds instead of every 5 minutes — much more useful for detecting stress spikes during trades.
